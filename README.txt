in order to Transfer your Spotify plalist into your tidal Account, do as follows:

1. open www.exportify.net (Hyperlink in spotidal Folder) and sign into your Spotify Account.
2. after logging in, you might Need to press "get started" on exportify.net. all your Playlists should load - if not, reload the page.
3. download the csv-file for the Playlist to be transferred into the spotidal folder. a download will start with the Name of your Playlist.
4. open the SpotidalGUI.exe in the dist Folder. the program Pops up.
5. choose the csv file of your Playlist. Name the Playlist.
6. press "Start Import". a browser window will Pop up asking you to Login to tidal. do so and confirm the code shown in your SpotidalGUI.exe
7. after successfully loggin in, SpotidalGUI.exe should start searching for the Songs.
8. once finished, statistics will Show how many Songs were found and which ones weren't.
9. the Playlist should appear in your tidal Account.
